NHS WELL_LAB : INTRO VIDEO
←
→

An introductory video for a UK-based organisation, Well Lab. Well Lab aims to tackle burnout in the workplace with clients such as the NHS and UCL. The organisation primarily works with employers and organizations who are looking to support their employees' mental health and well-being. Well Lab’s branding is characterised by a minimalist design that utilises simple shapes and a muted, pastel colour palette. There is a focus on conveying a sense of authority, competence, and practical support for those struggling with burnout.

Following the organisation’s brand guidelines, the studio took the shapes and colour palette provided and translated it into a series of whimsical and dynamic movements with workers and scientists being represented as coloured circles falling in and out of balance. The video aims to capture the interest of potential partners by differentiating themselves from other firms by utilising sleek, modern design and creating a focus on the abstraction of human representation.

Voiceover and branding guidelines (including shapes and colour palette) provided by Well Lab.

SPURGEONS : DISORDERED EATING AWARENESS CAMPAIGN
←
→

A set of five videos and around sixty illustrations were made for UK-based children’s charity, Spurgeons. The charity was founded in 1867 and has supported vulnerable children and families for over 150 years by providing a range of services including support programs, parenting courses, child and family therapy, and children's centers. This particular campaign was launched for Eating Disorder Awareness Week and aims to bring attention to eating disorders in teenagers and children.

Spurgeons' branding is designed to convey a sense of warmth, approachability, and compassion, which reflects the charity's mission to support some of the most vulnerable members of society. This translated into a series of inclusive, friendly-looking characters of all races, sizes and disabilities. The videos themselves were also designed to be easy to understand and accessible for parents of all ages. Through a series of five videos, each around five minutes in length, the characters can be seen interacting with each other by talking, eating together, going for walks as well as being in situations of distress such as arguing. Teenagers are seen having a complicated relationship with their bodies by over-eating, bingeing, purging and overexercising. Overall, the video aims to shed light on a serious matter while being inclusive and easy to comprehend.

Voiceover and branding guidelines provided by Spurgeons. Original illustrations provided by illustrator upklyak.

AMZSITE PROMOTIONAL VIDEOS
←
→

A promotion video was created for US-based service provider, AMZSite. AMZsite offers a range of tools and solutions for Amazon sellers. Their services include listing optimization, keyword research, product research, and competitor analysis, among others. This video aims to quickly capture the attention of potential clients, who are primarily Amazon sellers looking to optimize their product listings and improve their sales performance.

The video conveys the company's origin, purpose and current statistics as well as it’s future plans. A collaboration with the firm's CEO, the four-minute video strays from the company's usual clean, white branding and instead utilises strong, neon colours on a dark background (often associated with high reward establishments such as casinos) complimented by quick text pops in striking white to keep the viewer informed and entertained. Aiming to differentiate themselves from other service providers in the crowded Amazon selling market, the video aims to utilise a neon, night-time theme (often associated with high energy, excitement, and vibrancy) to create a sense of urgency or excitement around their service.

Voiceover and branding guidelines provided by AMZSite.
